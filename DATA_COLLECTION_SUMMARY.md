## ⚡ Quick Setup:

1. **Go to https://supabase.com**
2. **Create free account** (GitHub/Google login)
3. **Create project** (takes 1-2 minutes)
4. **Copy credentials:**
   - Project URL
   - Anon Key
5. **Paste into config.js**
6. **Run SQL table creation** (provided in SUPABASE_GUIDE.md)
7. **Done!** 🎉
